Welcome to the World of Pandas

Pandas are beloved animals known for their distinctive black and white coloring. They are native to China and primarily eat bamboo. Below is a list of interesting facts about pandas:

  • Pandas spend about 12 hours a day eating bamboo.
  • They have a special bone that acts like a thumb, helping them grasp bamboo stalks.
  • Pandas are solitary animals and have their own territories.

Learn more about pandas by visiting the WWF Panda Page.

Pandas in the Wild

Pandas are considered a vulnerable species, and efforts are being made to protect their habitats and increase their population. Conservation programs and protected reserves have helped improve their numbers in recent years.
